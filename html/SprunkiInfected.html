Overview Sprunki Infected 

Sprunki Infected is a captivating puzzle game where you control a mischievous Sprunki, a soda-loving creature, through a series of challenging levels. Your mission is to collect all the Sprunkies and defeat the infected ones before time runs out.

How to Play

  • Control Sprunki: Use the mouse or keyboard to move Sprunki left, right, up, and down.
  • Collect Sprunkies: Search for healthy Sprunkies scattered throughout the levels. Collect them to increase your score.
  • Defeat Infected Sprunkies: Infected Sprunkies will try to harm you. Avoid them or defeat them by jumping on their heads.
  • Beat the Clock: Each level has a time limit. Collect all the healthy Sprunkies and defeat the infected ones before the time expires.

Gameplay

Sprunki Infected offers a fast-paced and addictive gameplay experience. The levels are designed to be both challenging and rewarding, with a focus on quick reflexes and problem-solving. The game's colorful graphics and catchy music create a fun and energetic atmosphere.

Mouse and Keyboard Guide

  • Mouse: Move the mouse to control Sprunki's direction.
  • Keyboard: Use the arrow keys to move Sprunki left, right, up, and down.
