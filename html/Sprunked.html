Overview of Sprunked

Sprunked is a fan-made mod of the popular music-mixing game Incredibox. The original game lets players create music tracks by combining different beatbox, vocal, and instrumental loops. In Sprunked, the experience is expanded or altered with custom sounds, visuals, and themes that deviate from the standard versions. The mod provides a unique spin on the original concept, offering fresh sound combinations and visual styles that cater to fans looking for a different music-mixing experience.

How to Play Sprunked

Drag and Drop Sounds: Players start with a selection of icons representing different sound elements, such as beats, melodies, and effects.

Create Combinations: Drag and drop the icons onto character avatars to trigger the sounds. The aim is to combine sounds to create a cohesive music track.

Unlock Bonuses: Achieve special combinations to unlock bonus animations or songs, which can enhance the music mix and visuals.

Share Your Mix: Once you’re satisfied with the track, you can share it with others or save it for future listening.
