What is Sprunki But I Ruined It

The Sprunki But I Ruined It game is a platform game where the player controls a character named Sprunki, who must navigate through a series of levels to reach the goal. The levels are filled with various obstacles, such as enemies, traps, and moving platforms.

How to Play Sprunki But I Ruined It

The game is played using the mouse and keyboard. The mouse is used to move Sprunki, and the keyboard is used to jump and interact with objects.

Gameplay

The gameplay of Sprunki But I Ruined It is simple. The player must guide Sprunki through the levels without falling off any platforms or being hit by enemies. The levels are designed to be challenging, and the player will need to use all of their skills to reach the goal.

Instructions for Using Mouse and Keyboard

  • Move Sprunki: Use the left and right mouse buttons to move Sprunki left and right.
  • Jump: Press the spacebar to jump.
  • Interact with objects: Press the E key to interact with objects, such as doors and switches.
